Travel Warnings: No-Fly Zones

Please check these restricted areas before planning your trip!

Restricted Airspace over Military Bases

Flying drones or private aircraft over military areas is strictly prohibited.

Conflict Zones

Regions under conflict or sanctions may be unsafe for travel or flights.

Wildfire Affected Areas

Air travel may be restricted in regions affected by wildfires or severe smoke.

Volcanic Eruption Zones

Flying over active volcanoes may pose serious hazards to aircraft.

Help & Safety

Important tips and contacts to keep your travels safe.

Women Safety Tips

  • Share your itinerary with someone you trust.
  • Prefer traveling in groups or with a companion at night.
  • Keep emergency numbers saved on your phone.
  • Avoid isolated areas and poorly lit streets.
  • Use registered cabs or ride-sharing services.

Important Contacts (India)

  • Women Helpline: 1091
  • Police Emergency: 100
  • Medical Emergency: 102 / 108
  • Disaster Management Helpline: 1070
  • Tourist Helpline: 1363
